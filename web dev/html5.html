NIELIT Imphal Centre is centre of NIELIT New Delhi, which is an autonomous body of Department of Electronics and Information Technology, Ministry of Communication & Information Technology, Govt. of India. NIELIT Imphal is participating in Nation Building by conducting various long term & short term skill development courses in the fields of Electronics & Communication Engineering and Information Technology.
NIELIT Imphal also offers various short term courses in the skill development sector with an objective of enhancing the employability of the youth of the state and country as a whole as mandated by the Government of India under the National Skill Development policy.
